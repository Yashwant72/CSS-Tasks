FIFA WORLD CUP NEWS 2014

The 2014 FIFA World Cup was the 20th FIFA World Cup, the quadrennial world championship for men's national football teams organised by FIFA. It took place in Brazil from 12 June-13

July 2014, after the country was awarded the hosting rights in 2007. It was the second time that Brazil staged the competition, the first being in 1950, and the fifth time that it was held in

South America. 31 national teams advanced through qualification competitions to join the host nation in the final tournament and 64 matches were played in 12 venues located Brazil main cities.
